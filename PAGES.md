# Traveloop — Page Manifest

Each page in Traveloop is designed with a "Luxury-Minimal" aesthetic to provide a high-end, editorial experience for the modern explorer.

---

### 1. Login (`/login`)
The gateway to the Traveloop mission control, featuring a high-fidelity dark UI, signature circular branding, and secure credential entry designed to create an immediate "Command Center" atmosphere.

### 2. Register (`/register`)
A premium onboarding experience where new agents can establish their identity, including full profile metadata and tactical photo uploads, mirroring the layout of high-end bespoke SaaS platforms.

### 3. Mission Control Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
The primary operational hub, offering an asymmetrical editorial view of trip statistics, active itineraries, and personalized "Field Briefings" powered by real-time relational analytics.

### 4. Trip Archive (`/trips`)
A systematic collection of every journey and expedition curated by your travel loop, featuring a tabbed management system (Active, Planned, Archived) and high-fidelity, interactive trip cards.

### 5. Create Expedition (`/trips/new`)
A sophisticated multi-field orchestration form for initiating new travel missions, including budget allocation and timeline definition with strict Zod-based data validation.

### 6. Trip Overview (`/trips/[id]`)
A comprehensive command briefing for a specific travel loop, provide quick tactical access to the Itinerary Builder, Fiscal Ledger, Supply Checklist, and Tactical Journal.

### 7. Itinerary Engine (`/trips/[id]/build`)
An interactive, motion-heavy engine for managing trip stops and time-blocked activities, utilizing expandable stop canvases and real-time database reordering for precise planning.

### 8. Tactical Supply Checklist (`/trips/[id]/checklist`)
A functional supply manager with real-time progress tracking, category-based grouping, and optimistic UI updates to ensure agents are 100% ready before deployment.

### 9. Trip Journal (`/trips/[id]/notes`)
A masonry-style, editorial logbook for capturing travel memories and important briefings, allowing agents to filter their thoughts by mission day or specific stop.

### 10. Fiscal Ledger & Invoicing (`/trips/[id]/invoice`)
A professional financial suite that automatically calculates grand totals, VAT (5%) estimation, and budget deltas, presented as a high-fidelity, export-ready travel invoice.

### 11. Social Society (`/community`)
A global social uplink for sharing intelligence and discovering successful travel routes, featuring a real-time feed with community interactions and trending coordinate tracking.

### 12. Intelligence Search (`/search`)
A full-screen, high-impact search experience for querying the global database of 10,000+ landmarks and activities, designed for rapid data collection during the planning phase.

### 13. Personnel Profile (`/profile`)
A high-end identity dashboard displaying agent metrics, mission history, and system settings in a luxury-minimal layout with glassmorphic depth effects.

### 14. Admin Control Center (`/admin`)
A restricted command center for system administrators, providing real-time data visualization on user growth, system health, and popular global coordinates.

### 15. Shared Itinerary (`/shared/[slug]`)
A read-only, high-fidelity public landing page for sharing itineraries with external parties via unique tactical slugs, optimized for high-impact visual presentation.
